The Man Behind the Art

Emmanuel Torres (a.k.a. Static) is a self-trained graphic artist and photographer.


His designs have been featured on Mixtapes from underground artists all over Atlanta.


Check out the gallery to see his designs.





About the Designer

Static began his career as a musician, performing at clubs and bars in the Atlanta area. His rap and reggaeton beats were so popular that other rappers began to ask him to make beats.

Producing music led him to discover an even bigger talent: designing art to showcase his mixtapes

Demand for his art was even higher than demand for his beats, leading Static to devote his career to photography and design.
